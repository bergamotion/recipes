Pizza

Description

Hello my little fellas! Today we are going to cook pizza according to my granmother's recipe.

Ingredients

  • 500g white flour
  • 400g can plum tomatoes
  • 50g parmesan
  • 200g mozzarella

Steps

  1. First, make the dough
  2. Now make the tomate sauce
  3. To make the pizza, heat a grill to its highest setting and get a heavy frying pan.
  4. Get the pan very hot and carefully slide the pizza onto it.
